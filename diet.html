Diet and Health

My animal-based diet

As a former protein enthusiast, I had the mindset that everything "high-protein" is healthy; however, I was compeletly wrong. I used to consume ultra-processed food like protein shakes, chips, and even protein cookies. After doing this habit for months, I discovered the harsh reality of ultra-processed foods. On instagram, many influencers were bringing up the controversy behind seed oils and chemicals in food. With all this in mind, I gradually switched to an animal-based diet which consisted of fruits, raw diary, raw honey, and a variety of meats. With this diet, I not only felt better, but played at my best in tennis. My acne cleared and my cooking skills got better every time. One of the influencers that started me on this journey was Paul Saladino, an animal-based certified board MD.

Many consider red meat to be cancerous and a carcinogen; I disagree with this. 100% grass red meat contains bioavailable protein and vitamins plus minerals that are essential for the body. Along with this, red meat contains good saturated fat which raises your good cholestral. Remember, there is a HUGE difference between a processed sausage or salami vs a 100% grass-fed steak. Our ancestors have been eating meat a lot in the past along with fruits which occured naturally in nature.
